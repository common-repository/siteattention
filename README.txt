== Installation ==
1. Check requirements – WP version must be at least 4.5.x and at most at version 4.9.5 (might work for higher versions, tested until 4.9.5). PHP must be at least version 5.4 – If in doubt, ask your server-host.
2. Go to “Plugins” tab, find SiteAttention and click on “Activate” button underneath, then click on “Settings” (you can also click on SiteAttention tab in the main WordPress menu) which will take you to the SiteAttention setup form.
3. In the “New customer” section write down your first name and email address, then click on Get started!
4. Congratulations! Now you can start using your SiteAttention plugin! Simply go to the post or page editor and the plugin will appear by the page you’re editing, giving you SEO tips in real-time!
